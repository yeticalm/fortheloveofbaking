Our Story

♥

Welcome to For the Love of Baking! We’re a mother-and-daughter bakery built on a shared passion for baking — and a commitment to treats that everyone can enjoy.

Baking has been part of my life for over 15 years. What began as a creative outlet — sharing homemade desserts with family and friends — became something much more meaningful when, a few years ago, my daughter had to transition to a gluten-free diet for medical reasons.

Like many families navigating dietary restrictions, we quickly discovered that gluten-free baked goods that truly tasted great were hard to find. We missed the flavors, the textures, and the joy that real baked goods bring.

Instead of settling for less, we went back to the kitchen.

Together we spent countless hours experimenting with ingredients, testing recipes, and refining techniques — with plenty of taste testing along the way. What started as a need became a passion, and we developed recipes that are not just gluten-free, but good enough to be enjoyed by everyone.

Today, For the Love of Baking combines years of baking experience with our family’s own journey. Every item is made with care, quality ingredients, and attention to detail.

What We Bake

Everything we make is 100% gluten-free. Our current lineup:

Moist, tender loaf cakes in rotating flavors — double chocolate, lemon glaze, and more — ideal for sharing, gifting, or keeping entirely to yourself.
